Program Overview

The Bachelor's in Design at Sykli College is a forward-thinking program that blends creative excellence with technical proficiency. Students explore the intersection of aesthetic principles, user-centric design, and sustainable innovation. Through hands-on studio projects, collaborative workshops, and industry integration, the program prepares the next generation of designers to solve complex problems across digital and physical mediums.
