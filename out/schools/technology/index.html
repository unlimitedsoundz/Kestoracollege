Academic Departments

Organised into specialized departments driving innovation and research excellence.

Department of Automation & Control Engineering

The Department of Automation & Control Engineering at SYKLI College focuses on intelligent systems that enable machines, processes, and industries to operate efficiently, safely, and autonomously.

Head of Department

Dr. Lars Suzuki

Head of Department of Automation & Control Engineering

Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering

The department combines strong engineering foundations with practical, application-driven learning. Programmes cover electrical engineering, electronics engineering, power systems, nanoengineering, embedded systems, and automation technologies.

Head of Department

Dr. Väinö Brown

Head of Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Department of Energy and Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Energy and Mechanical Engineering focuses on sustainable energy conversion and machine design. We cover thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and marine technology.

Head of Department

Dr. Silvia Kemppainen

Head of Energy and Mechanical Engineering

Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering

Programmes cover civil engineering, structural engineering, construction management, environmental engineering, and sustainable infrastructure systems. Students engage in practical learning through design projects, fieldwork, and industry-linked case studies that address real challenges in the built and natural environment.

Head of Department

Dr. Toivo Andersen

Head of Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering
