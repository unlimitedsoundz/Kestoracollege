Academic Departments

Organised into specialized departments driving innovation and research excellence.

Department of Marketing

The Department of Marketing is at the forefront of consumer research and brand strategy. We explore customer behavior, digital marketing, and market analytics. Our curriculum encourages creativity and data-driven decision-making. Students learn to build strong brands, design customer-centric experiences, and develop marketing strategies that drive growth and engagement in competitive markets.

Head of Department

Dr. Sari Schröder

Department of Management Studies

The Department of Management Studies explores the human side of business. We focus on organizational behavior, leadership, strategy, and international business.

Head of Department

Dr. Sarah Dubois

Head of Management Studies

Department of Business

The Department of Business at SYKLI College focuses on developing future-ready professionals with strong analytical, leadership, and entrepreneurial skills. The department blends business theory with real-world practice, preparing students to navigate modern organizations, markets, and global economies. Programmes within the department emphasize management, marketing, finance, accounting, entrepreneurship, and business law, with a strong focus on innovation, sustainability, and ethical decision-making.

Head of Department

Dr. Kasper Hernandez

Head of Business

Department of Economics

Our Department of Economics is dedicated to understanding the mechanisms that drive prosperity and well-being. We offer deep training in micro- and macroeconomics, econometrics, and policy analysis.

Head of Department

Dr. Noah Väisänen

Head of Economics

Department of Information and Service Management

In the Department of Information and Service Management, we bridge the gap between business strategy and technology. Our focus areas include business analytics, supply chain management, and information systems.

Head of Department

Dr. Henrik Wagner

Head of Information and Service Management

Department of Accounting & Business Law

The Department of Accounting & Business Law offers a rigorous foundation in financial reporting, auditing, and corporate governance. We emphasize ethics, transparency, and the critical role of accounting in the global economy.

Head of Department

Dr. Thomas Ivanov

Head of Accounting & Business Law

Department of Finance

The Department of Finance provides advanced education in asset pricing, corporate finance, and risk management. We combine theoretical rigor with practical application, utilizing modern financial tools and market data.

Head of Department

Dr. Adrian Nielsen

Head of Finance
