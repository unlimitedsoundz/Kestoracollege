Business

The Department of Business at SYKLI College focuses on developing future-ready professionals with strong analytical, leadership, and entrepreneurial skills. The department blends business theory with real-world practice, preparing students to navigate modern organizations, markets, and global economies. Programmes within the department emphasize management, marketing, finance, accounting, entrepreneurship, and business law, with a strong focus on innovation, sustainability, and ethical decision-making.
